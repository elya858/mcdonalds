About the "Open Doors" Program

McDonald’s invites you to take a closer look at our kitchen! Discover how your favorite meals are prepared and see for yourself how we ensure the highest
standards of quality and safety. This program is a unique opportunity to learn more about real people, real processes, and the real McDonald’s.

The tour will be conducted in Azerbaijani.

Only guests aged 16 and above are allowed to participate.

Note! Before submitting your application, you must review the document “Mətbəx ziyarətçisinin sağlamlıq bəyannaməsi”. This form will be provided at your
chosen restaurant and must be signed before the start of the tour.

View the “Mətbəx ziyarətçisinin sağlamlıq bəyannaməsi”

When filling out the application, all fields must be completed fully and correctly. During registration, you need to select your preferred restaurant and date. On
the day of the tour, please arrive at the restaurant at least 15 minutes before the start time. Upon arrival, ask the shift manager — they will register you and
provide a short briefing before the tour.

We wish you an enjoyable and memorable time at our restaurant!

We'll never share your email with anyone else.
